No Bake Cookies

This recipe is quick and simple way to make some no bake cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups of peanut butter

Steps

  1. mix oatmeal, sugar, and peanut butter in a bowl
  2. mix until all ingredients are combined
  3. roll a small ball of dough
  4. place ball on pan
  5. place in fride
  6. wait 30 minutes to cool
