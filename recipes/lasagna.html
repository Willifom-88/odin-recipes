Cheesy Italian Lasagna

This recipe is quick and simple, taking only 1 hour total to complete a cheesy Italian Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cheese
  • package of noodles
  • 1 jar sauce
  • 2 cups of meat

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 250 degrees
  2. combine meat, and sauce in a bowl
  3. lay noodles out in pan
  4. pour meat and sauce on noodles
  5. place more noodles on top
  6. add cheese
  7. place in oven for 60 min.
